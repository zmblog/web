Website Abstract

This website is essentially a personal page, dedicated to a weekly blog which documents my pursuits this summer; from algebra to real analysis and codeforces to flask and django, I hope to spend my summer learning and improving my skills in a wide range of areas in mathematics and computer science (Yes I am aware I am 20 days into it already). Note that the formatting and style of this webpage comes from this GitHub link, which I somehow randomly came accross when searching how to convert a pdf not in Latex formatting, well, to Latex formatting lol. It was one of the main reasons I was inspired to do this tbh. If you can't tell already, I like Latex very much.
